FAQ

What is this website used for?

This website is a place for everyone to enjoy their favourite music, pictures, and videos. Every user can upload, use and download media files. Files are saved in your raspberry pi and backed up in cloud servers.

Who wrote this website?

This website is written by students in Room 466 in Dormitory 32, ZJU, including Zeng Benchong, Xu Lvtao, Mai Jiaming and Yang Haotian.

How can I upload my media?

Just login in and click "upload", choose files, and wait for a minute, then you can see your files on the website.

Why some videos don't have a download button?

Videos without download buttons are provided by special users who have the copyright of them. You can watch them free, but you are unable to download until you buy them.

Why there are watermarks on some pictures?

Pictures with watermarks are provided by special users who have the copyright of them. Click "buy" and you can get these pictures without watermarks.

Features

Great features you'll love.

Our website can create a wonderful experience of enjoying different kinds of media.

Have Fun With Your Family

You can set a group for your family. Some files can be shared among the family, while other files can be kept personal, which are visible only for the uploader of them.

Buying Legitimate Media

Only reliable content providers can become our special users. We make sure that the media provided by special users are all legitimate.

Access Management

There are 3 levels for our access management: 1.Only the uploader can see. 2.Only users in a certain group can see. 3.Every user can see.

Adding Watermarks

Special users can use our auto watermarks to prevent their files from free downloading.

Safe Storage

The uploaded files are saved in your raspberry pi and in the cloud at the same time. So you do not need to worry that your media may be lost.

LED lights

With an LED screen connected to your raspberry pi, you can see the screen changes while a video or a song is playing.
